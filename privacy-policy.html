Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 18, 2024

1. Introduction

Welcome to Praxen Timer ("we," "our," or "us"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we handle your data when you use our timer application.

2. Information We Collect

We collect and process the following types of information:

  • Timer Configurations: Your saved timer settings and configurations
  • Usage Data: How you interact with the app, including features used and preferences
  • Device Information: Basic information about your device and app version for troubleshooting

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain the timer functionality
  • To save and restore your timer configurations
  • To improve the app's performance and user experience
  • To provide technical support when needed
  • To send important updates about the app

4. Data Storage and Security

Your timer configurations are stored:

  • Locally on your device
  • In iCloud (if enabled) for backup and sync purposes

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your data against unauthorized access or alteration.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties. Your timer configurations remain private unless you choose to share them.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Delete your data from the app
  • Export your timer configurations
  • Opt-out of data collection

7. Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
